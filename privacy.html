Introduction

Stellar Strike ("the Game") is developed by Sven Delporte. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Game.

Information we collect

The Game itself does not directly collect any personal information. All game data — scores, achievements, progress — is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

However, the Game uses third-party services that may collect information.

Third-party advertising

The Game uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use the following data to serve personalized ads:

  • Device identifiersSuch as the advertising identifier (IDFA).
  • Approximate locationCoarse location data based on IP address.
  • Usage dataApp interactions and information about advertisements viewed.
  • DiagnosticsCrash data and performance data for SDK stability.

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings under Settings › Privacy & Security › Tracking.

Data retention

The Game does not store any personal data on external servers. All gameplay data is stored locally on your device and can be removed by deleting the app.

Children's privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ad content is served by Google AdMob in accordance with their policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this policy.
